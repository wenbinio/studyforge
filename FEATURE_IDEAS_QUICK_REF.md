## ‚ú® What StudyForge Can Do BETTER

### 1. **True All-in-One Integration**
- Competitors make you choose tools. StudyForge unifies everything.
- **Add:** "Study Flow" AI that suggests next action based on context
- **Add:** Cross-module analytics (e.g., "best quiz scores after 50-min Pomodoros")

### 2. **AI-First, Not AI-Bolted-On**
- Competitors added AI as afterthought. StudyForge built with it.
- **Add:** AI Study Coach (personalized daily plan)
- **Add:** Natural language commands ("create 10 hard flashcards about torts")
- **Add:** AI adaptive difficulty (like Duolingo)

### 3. **Law Student Specialization**
- Only app with hypotheticals, essay grading, participation questions.
- **Add:** Case brief templates with citation extraction
- **Add:** IRAC framework tool
- **Add:** Bar exam prep mode
- **Add:** Cold call practice
- **Market:** Partner with law schools, charge premium tier

### 4. **Privacy & Local-First**
- No cloud vendor lock-in. Data stays on device.
- **Add:** Optional end-to-end encrypted sync
- **Market:** "Academic Honor Code Compliant"
- **Market:** GDPR/FERPA certifications

### 5. **Fair Pricing**
- No predatory subscriptions (Quizlet = $20/mo).
- **Propose:** Free tier (core features) + Pro ($5/mo) + Lifetime ($200)
- **Propose:** Student discount (50% with .edu email)
